Season 2

Light and Shadow

The Story Continues

Set three years after the tragic events of Season 1, Jang Uk returns from death as a dark and hardened hunter of soul-shifters. He bears the immense power of the Ice Stone but is tormented by the wraiths of those he has killed.

When he encounters a mysterious amnesiac prisoner in Jinyowon named Jin Bu-yeon, their fates intertwine. She asks for his help to escape her magical imprisonment, unaware of her true identity and the deep past they share.
